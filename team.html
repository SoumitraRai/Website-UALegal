Meet Our Legal Team

Our team combines expertise and dedication to provide exceptional legal services across India. We are committed to excellence and delivering results for our clients.

Founder

Aditya Dhawan

Founder
LL.M., Punjab University, Chandigarh

Born in Faridabad, Haryana. Mr. Dhawan has worked with the Chairman of Bar Council of Delhi, trained in litigation at Delhi District Court, and assisted Shri A.S. Chandhiok (Former ASG) in drafting the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016. He also has experience in Real Estate Investment Funds and has appeared before various Tribunals and Authorities.

Philosophy: Focused on timely justice and effective resolution.
Motto: "निष्ठां घृतीः सत्यं" – Dedication, Steadfastness, and Truth.

Our Team

UA Legal has a network of highly experienced lawyers across all major commercial cities in India. Our team includes qualified Insolvency Resolution Professionals, Company Secretaries, and lawyers who specialize in various areas of law.

Our team has extensive experience across judicial and quasi-judicial forums including:

  • Supreme Court of India
  • High Courts
  • National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT)
  • Appellate Tribunals
  • Tax Tribunals
  • National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission
  • Competition Commission
  • Electricity and Telecom Disputes Tribunals
  • Other Adjudicating Authorities
