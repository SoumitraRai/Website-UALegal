Founder's Message

UA Legal is a premier Indian law firm headquartered in New Delhi. Established in 2020, our goal is to serve the corporate world, particularly small and medium industries that often lack legal resources and awareness. We provide personalized service and commitment to ensure client satisfaction. Our reputation, coupled with our negotiation and litigation strengths, enables us to deliver results effectively. Our founder, Mr. Aditya Dhawan, brings exceptional experience and skills to the firm. UA Legal has a network of highly experienced lawyers across all major commercial cities in India. We are equipped to handle both domestic and cross-border transactions. Our team includes qualified Insolvency Resolution Professionals, Company Secretaries, and lawyers. We specialize in litigation, dispute resolution, and corporate insolvency and bankruptcy matters, and have extensive experience across judicial and quasi-judicial forums including: Supreme Court of India High Courts National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) Appellate Tribunals Tax Tribunals National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission Competition Commission Electricity and Telecom Disputes Tribunals Other Adjudicating Authorities
